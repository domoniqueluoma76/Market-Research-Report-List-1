<p><p>The Non-Silicone Antifoam Market encompasses two primary types: water-based and oil-based antifoams. Conversely, oil-based antifoams utilize hydrophobic oils to eliminate foam effectively in industrial processes, such as petrochemicals and textiles, where increased stability and durability are essential. Both types offer distinct advantages tailored to specific industry needs.</p></p>

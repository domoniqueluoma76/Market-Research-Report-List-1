<p><p>The pharmaceutical packaging machines market encompasses three primary types: primary packaging machines, secondary packaging machines, and labeling and serialization machines. Primary packaging machines focus on direct product containment, ensuring sterility and protection. Secondary packaging machines handle bulk packaging for distribution, enhancing product safety during transit. Labeling machines apply essential information on packaging, while serialization machines ensure regulatory compliance by tracking and tracing products. Together, these machines play a crucial role in maintaining product integrity, safety, and compliance in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>

<p><p>The Crosslinked Polyethylene (PEX) tube market is segmented into three main types: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa is produced through the continuous.Method known for its high degree of crosslinking and flexibility, making it ideal for hot and cold water applications. PE-Xb is created using a chemical process that introduces crosslinking, offering good thermal stability and mechanical properties. PE-Xc utilizes irradiation to achieve crosslinking, providing robust resistance to temperature and pressure, suitable for heating systems and plumbing.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>PPP</li><li>RTK</li><li>RTK-PPP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hypergeolocation Service market is categorized into three main types: PPP (Post-Processed Kinematic), RTK (Real-Time Kinematic), and RTK-PPP (Real-Time Kinematic with Post-Processed capabilities). PPP provides precise positioning by processing data after collection, while RTK offers real-time positioning accuracy for applications like surveying and autonomous vehicles. RTK-PPP combines both methods, delivering enhanced real-time accuracy with post-processing benefits, making it suitable for dynamic environments. Together, these services cater to diverse industries requiring high-precision geolocation solutions.</p></p>

<p><p>Cloud infrastructure services are categorized into three main types: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. Public Cloud offers shared resources over the internet, allowing multiple organizations to access scalable services at lower costs. Private Cloud, on the other hand, provides dedicated infrastructure for a single organization, enhancing control and security. Hybrid Cloud combines elements of both, enabling organizations to utilize public resources while keeping sensitive data on private infrastructure, thus offering flexibility and scalability tailored to varied needs.</p></p>

<p><p>Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) is a versatile insulation material used in construction and industrial applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: open cell and closed cell foam. Open cell foam is lightweight, flexible, and provides sound absorption, making it ideal for interior applications. Closed cell foam, on the other hand, offers higher insulation values, moisture resistance, and structural support, suitable for exterior applications. Additionally, other specialty formulations cater to specific needs, enhancing energy efficiency and durability.</p></p>

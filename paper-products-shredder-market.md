<p><ul><li>Strip Cut</li><li>Cross Cut</li><li>Micro Cut</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper products shredder market consists of three main types: strip cut, cross cut, and micro cut. Strip cut shredders slice paper into long, narrow strips, offering basic security for everyday documents. Cross cut shredders provide a higher level of security by cutting paper into small rectangular pieces, making it more difficult to reconstruct the original content. Micro cut shredders offer the highest level of security, transforming paper into tiny particles, ideal for sensitive information protection in commercial and personal settings.</p></p>

<p><p>The battery market for medical machines encompasses various types, each with unique characteristics. Lithium-Ion (Li-Ion) batteries are popular for their high energy density and lightweight design, enabling longer device usage. Nickel Cadmium (Ni-Cd) batteries are known for their reliability but suffer from memory effect, impacting capacity. Nickel Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries offer improved performance and capacity over Ni-Cd. Alkaline-Manganese batteries provide a cost-effective solution for low-drain devices. Other battery types may also be employed, depending on specific medical applications and requirements.</p></p>

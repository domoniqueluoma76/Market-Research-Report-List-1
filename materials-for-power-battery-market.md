<p><p>The power battery market encompasses various materials essential for battery performance. Cathode materials, like lithium nickel manganese cobalt oxide, enable energy storage and discharge efficiency. Anode materials, typically graphite or silicon, influence charge capacity and cycle life. Lithium-ion battery separators provide a barrier between electrodes, preventing short circuits while allowing ion flow. Lastly, electrolytes facilitate ion movement between anode and cathode, impacting overall battery performance and stability. Each component plays a critical role in the functionality and longevity of power batteries.</p></p>

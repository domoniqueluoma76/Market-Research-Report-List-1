<p><p>Coalescers are devices used to separate liquids from a mixture, primarily in the oil and gas, petrochemical, and chemical industries. Mechanical coalescers rely on physical processes, utilizing filtration and gravity to combine smaller droplets into larger ones for easier separation. In contrast, electrostatic coalescers use electrical fields to enhance droplet aggregation and separation efficiency. Both types effectively remove impurities and improve product quality, catering to different operational needs and efficiencies in various industrial applications.</p></p>
